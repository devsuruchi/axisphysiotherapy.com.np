Clinical Background

Dr. Ashmita Shrestha is an accomplished Consultant Neuro Physiotherapist. She earned her MPT in Neurology from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), India, where she graduated in the First Division with academic Distinction.

With clinical expertise spanning prestigious institutions in both Nepal and India, Dr. Shrestha specializes in treating complex neuromuscular conditions and post-surgical restorations using an evidence-based approach.
