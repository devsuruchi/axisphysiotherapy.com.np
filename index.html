Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

No, you do not need a referral to book an appointment with us. As licensed physiotherapy professionals, we are trained first-contact practitioners. This means we have the clinical expertise to independently assess, screen, and diagnose musculoskeletal and neurological conditions. However, some insurance providers still require a doctor's note before they will reimburse the costs. It’s always best to check your specific policy first.
Look for credentials. A licensed professional will usually have a degree (like a BPT, MPT, DPT) and must be registered with the **NHPC (Nepal Health Professional Council)**. You can usually verify their license number on the official NHPC website.
Every individual and condition is unique. During your initial assessment, we will provide a personalized treatment plan with an estimated timeline. While minor strains may show significant improvement in 3 to 6 sessions, chronic neurological or post-surgical conditions often require a longer, consistent rehabilitation program to reach your goals.
Our goal is to reduce your pain, not increase it. While some manual techniques or new exercises may cause mild discomfort or "therapeutic soreness," we always work within your pain tolerance. Communication is key—we encourage you to tell us exactly how you are feeling during every treatment.
An initial assessment typically takes 30-40 mins to ensure a thorough diagnosis. Follow-up treatment sessions generally last between **1 hour to 1 hour 30 mins**, depending on the complexity of the treatment required.
