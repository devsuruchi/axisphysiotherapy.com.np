Clinical Narrative

Khushbu K.C. is a dedicated and compassionate physiotherapist with a strong academic foundation and diverse clinical experience across musculoskeletal, neurological, pediatric, and post-operative rehabilitation.

A graduate of Padmashree Institute of Physiotherapy (RGUHS), she achieved distinctions throughout her program. Currently a full-time therapist at Axis Physiotherapy, she specializes in evidence-based treatment plans that integrate manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and advanced electrotherapy.

Areas of Interest

Musculoskeletal & Sports

Pediatric Care

Specialized focus on Cerebral Palsy, Autism Spectrum Disorders, and developmental functional outcomes.

Neurological Rehab

Managing complex impairments and neurological conditions to enhance independence and quality of life.

Post-Operative

Evidence-based recovery protocols for total knee and hip replacements and other major orthopedic surgeries.
