Professional Profile

Dr. Pankesh Kumar Sharma is a highly skilled and dedicated Physiotherapist currently practicing at Axis Physiotherapy Pvt. Ltd.

With a clinical foundation built on a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and official verification from the Nepal Health Professional Council (NHPC), Dr. Sharma brings a sophisticated, evidence-based approach to physical rehabilitation and pain management.

Core Clinical Expertise

Dry Needling

Precision intramuscular stimulation used to deactivate trigger points, release chronic muscle tension, and accelerate the natural healing response.

Cupping Therapy

Utilizing myofascial decompression and vacuum therapy to improve local circulation, reduce inflammation, and enhance soft tissue mobility.

Chiropractic Adjustment

Expert application of spinal and joint manipulations designed to restore structural alignment, alleviate nerve impingement, and optimize biomechanical function.

Advanced Pain Recovery

Comprehensive care for complex cases involving chronic back pain, cervical spondylosis, sports injuries, and post-surgical rehabilitation.

Professional Philosophy

Addressing the root cause of dysfunction.

Dr. Sharma believes that every individual requires a bespoke treatment plan that addresses the root cause rather than merely suppressing symptoms.

Known for his clinical precision and compassionate communication, he empowers his patients through education and active rehabilitation, helping them regain their strength, mobility, and quality of life.
