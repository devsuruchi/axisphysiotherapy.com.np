Professional Profile

Dr. Pankesh Kumar Sharma is a highly skilled and dedicated Physiotherapist currently practicing at Axis Physiotherapy Pvt. Ltd.

With a clinical foundation built on a Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) and official verification from the Nepal Health Professional Council (NHPC), Dr. Sharma brings a sophisticated, evidence-based approach to physical rehabilitation and pain management.

Core Clinical Expertise

Dry Needling

Precision intramuscular stimulation used to release chronic muscle tension and accelerate natural healing response.

Cupping Therapy

Utilizing myofascial decompression to improve circulation, reduce inflammation, and enhance tissue mobility.

Chiropractic Adjustment

Expert application of spinal manipulations to restore structural alignment and optimize biomechanical function.

Pain Management

Comprehensive care for chronic back pain, cervical spondylosis, and specialized post-surgical rehabilitation.

Clinical Philosophy

Restoring the freedom to move without fear.

Dr. Sharma believes that every individual requires a bespoke treatment plan that addresses the root cause of dysfunction rather than merely suppressing symptoms.

Known for his clinical precision and compassionate communication, he empowers his patients through education and active rehabilitation, helping them regain their strength and quality of life.
