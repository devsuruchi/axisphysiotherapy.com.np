Professional Narrative

Dr. Pasang Doma Sherpa is a distinguished Consultant Neuro Physiotherapist with a robust international academic background. She earned both her Bachelor’s and Master’s (MPT) degrees from Sharda University, Greater Noida, India, establishing a deep foundation in neurological science.

This unique blend of analytical strategy and hands-on clinical mastery allows her to deliver superior patient-centered care at Axis Physiotherapy.
