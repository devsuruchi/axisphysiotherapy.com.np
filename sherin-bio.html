Professional Profile

Sherin Shrestha is a multidisciplinary professional with a strong foundation in architecture, finance, and entrepreneurship. She earned her degree in Architecture in 2009 and completed her MBA from the Asian Institute of Technology and European Business School in 2012.

She has built a diverse career across multinational corporations, the banking sector, and investment management. In 2021, she founded Axis Physiotherapy, demonstrating her entrepreneurial vision and leadership in the healthcare sector.
