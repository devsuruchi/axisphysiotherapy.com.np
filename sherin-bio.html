Executive Summary

Sherin Shrestha is a multidisciplinary professional with a strong foundation in architecture, finance, and entrepreneurship. She earned her degree in Architecture in 2009 and completed her MBA from the Asian Institute of Technology and European Business School in 2012.

She has built a diverse career across multinational corporations, the banking sector, and investment management, developing strong expertise in financial strategy and operations. In 2021, she co-founded Axis Physiotherapy in partnership with Dr. Suruchi Dev, demonstrating her entrepreneurial vision and leadership.
