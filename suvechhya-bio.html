Clinical Narrative

Dr. Suvechhya Bajracharya is an HCPC (UK) and NHPC-registered Physiotherapist with over 2 years of specialized experience in musculoskeletal, neurological, and post-operative rehabilitation.

During her clinical tenure at Axis Physiotherapy, she was recognized for her assessment and treatment of sports injuries and spinal conditions. She is an expert in APPI Clinical Pilates (Level 1 - 3), integrating core stability and postural correction into holistic recovery.

Current Status: United Kingdom

Dr. Suvechhya is currently residing in the United Kingdom, where she continues to practice physiotherapy at an international level. She is doing exceptionally well, applying the same clinical rigor and patient-centric care that she championed at Axis.

The entire team at Axis Physiotherapy is immensely proud of her achievements. We wish her continued success and a flourishing career in the UK.
