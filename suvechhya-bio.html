Professional Profile

Suvechhya Bajracharya is a dual-registered physiotherapist with accreditation from both the HCPC (UK) and the NHPC (Nepal). She brings over 2 years of specialized clinical experience across musculoskeletal, neurological, and post-operative rehabilitation.

Her practice is distinguished by the integration of APPI Clinical Pilates (Level 1 — 3), which she utilizes to improve core stability, postural alignment, and injury prevention for athletes and chronic pain patients alike.
